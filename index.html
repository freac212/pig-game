THE PIG GAME

RULES:

  1. The aim of the game is to have your total score reach the max game score
  2. Player 1 starts
  3. The player rolls both dice, and the total adds to their current score. The player can then choose to roll again, or hold
  4. If you chose to roll again, you risk getting a 1. If you get a 1 on either dice, your turn ends, you lose your points and it's now the second players turn
  5. If you chose to hold, your current score is then stored in your total score, and it becomes the other players turn.
  6. The round ends when a player reaches the Max Game Score (default:100)
